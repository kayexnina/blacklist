Blacklist International

Blacklist International emerged as a formidable force in the esports scene, particularly within the realm of Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB), a widely popular mobile multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game. Established in 2020, this professional esports organization hails from the Philippines and swiftly gained prominence in competitive gaming circles.

Throughout its journey, Blacklist International underwent roster changes, a common occurrence in esports, where players joined or departed to refine the team's performance and strategies. Despite these alterations, the team consistently showcased exceptional prowess and strategic depth in their gameplay.

Their list of achievements is notable, marked by stellar performances in various tournaments, including the Mobile Legends: Bang Bang Professional League (MPL) in the Philippines and other international events. Blacklist International secured victories, making deep runs in competitions, which highlighted their exceptional skill, cohesive teamwork, and adaptability to evolving meta changes within the game.

Earning a dedicated fan base, the team carved out a distinct identity within the esports community. Known for their strong camaraderie, strategic gameplay, and flexibility in adapting to shifting game dynamics, Blacklist International stood out as a force to be reckoned with in the competitive MLBB landscape.
